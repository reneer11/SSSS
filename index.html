Our Mission

Sunshine State Safe Spaces was created to connect Floridians with valuable resources for protecting themselves from contagious illnesses like COVID, the flu, and norovirus. This website offers easy access to information on safer events, businesses, mask blocs, and social groups in Florida and beyond. Designed as a collaborative space, it allows individuals across the state to contribute and share resources as they discover them.
